Rules:


1. Click "Roll Dice" to roll the dice.
2. Click as many times as you like to accumulate points.
3. If the dice result is 1, you lose all accumulated points, and it's the next player's turn.
4. To secure your points, click "Hold."
5. Once you click "Hold," it's the next player's turn.
6. The first player to reach 100 points wins.
7. Click "New Game" to restart the game.
